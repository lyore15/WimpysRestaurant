OUR HISTORY

Established in 1938, Wimpy’s Restaurant offers classic Cape Cod fare featuring fresh seafood and creative comfort cuisine. Our comfortable atmosphere includes a lively pub, 2 fireplace dining rooms and a Seafood Market with takeout. Located in the charming seaside village of Osterville, Wimpy’s strives to please each guest with the best dining experience possible.
